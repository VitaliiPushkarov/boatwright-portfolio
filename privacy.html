Privacy Policy

Your privacy is of utmost importance to us. This Privacy Policy details the information we collect, how we use it, and the steps we take to safeguard your personal information.

Information We Collect

We gather personal information through various interactions, including website visits, registrations, newsletter subscriptions, and other activities. The information we collect includes:

Personal Identification Information:

  • 1. Name
  • 2. Email Address
  • 3. Phone number
  • 4. Postal code

Non-Personal Identification Information:

  • 1. Browser type
  • 2. Operating system
  • 3. Internet Service Provider (ISP)
  • 4. IP address
  • 5. Date and time of visits
  • 6. Web pages viewed
  • 7. Time spent on site

How We Use Collected Information

Address customer service requests and support needs effectively.

Analyze user behavior to improve our services and site functionality.

Process orders and deliver services related to those orders without external sharing, except as necessary to fulfill the service.

Communicate order updates and respond to user inquiries, questions, or other requests.

Send periodic communications, such as newsletters, to users who opt-in to receive updates, including company news, updates, and relevant product or service information.

Cookie Policy

We are dedicated to maintaining your privacy and providing transparency about the technologies we use. This Cookie Policy explains the usage of cookies and similar technologies to enhance your user experience on our websites.

How We Protect Your Information

We employ appropriate data collection, storage, and processing practices along with security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Third-Party Links

Our website may include links to third-party websites or services that are not owned or controlled by our company. This Privacy Policy does not extend to these third-party sites, and we are not responsible for their privacy practices.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have specific rights concerning your personal information, such as accessing, correcting, deleting, or restricting the processing of your data. Contact us using the information provided below to exercise these rights.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. The updated policy will be posted on our website, and we recommend that you review it periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions regarding our Privacy Policy, please feel free to contact us at info@boatwright.co.za.
